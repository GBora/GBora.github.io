My Life

Born and raised in Alba Iulia, Romania.

I took my first steps as programmer here at the Mathemathics-Informatics class of the "Horia Closca and Crisan" highschool.

I continued my education at the Technical University of Cluj Napoca, where I got my bachelor's degree in Automatics and Applied Informatics.

I was lucky enough to receive an Erasmus scholarship to do my final paper at the Munchen Technical University.

My first job as a professional programmer was at Softvision, an IT firm here in Cluj.

It was a great opportunity for me as I could learn real software engineering practices within the context of large projects.

I mainly worked with C# specifically ASP.NET MVC but I also did a little front-end development mostly Javascript.

I currently work as a web developer at Pitech.

Due to the rise of SPA's the front-end of most site has become complex enough that I spend most of my time working front-end with a great emphasis on Javascript.

I am also leveraging my command of Javascript on the back-end with Node.js striving for the capabilities of a full stack developer.

One man cannot summon the future. But one man can change the present!
